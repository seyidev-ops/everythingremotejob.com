Real people. Real offers. Real dollars.

7-Week Repositioning
Chidi
Backend Engineer · Port Harcourt · 29
₦480,000/mo (~$310)$54,000/year via Deel

22 international applications in 5 months, 2 replies — both "US/EU only." His skills were never the problem; his pipeline was. We rebuilt his target list around companies with verified global hiring infrastructure, rewrote his GitHub and LinkedIn for remote-readiness, and moved to direct outreach. By week 7 he held two offers.

Same skills in week 1 as in week 7. What changed was aiming every action at companies already willing to hire him.

14-Month Career Pivot
Kemi
English Teacher → Technical Writer · Ibadan · 34
₦1.8M/yr (~$1,180)$58,000/year via Deel

Zero technical background. Seven years of explaining complex ideas to people who don't yet understand them — exactly what technical writing requires. Free courses, open-source documentation pull requests, first paid work at $35/hr, then a full-time offer from a UK developer-tools company.

"The 14 months felt very long while I was in them. Looking back, it was the shortest path to somewhere I genuinely wanted to be."

Negotiation Win · +$14,000
Taiwo
UX Designer · Abuja · 27
$38,000 offered$52,000accepted in 18 hours

One offer. Zero competing offers. "No leverage." What he had instead: a calculated salary floor, market data showing UK fintech UX roles at $48k–$62k, and a three-paragraph email that named the band, stayed honest, and made saying yes effortless. The hiring manager accepted without a counter.

The email took 25 minutes to write. Over a three-year tenure, the difference compounds to $42,000.

8-Week Turnaround
Blessing
NGO Project Coordinator → Remote PM · Lagos · 31
312 applications · 0 replies$56,000/year via Deel

Eleven months, 312 tracked applications, 308 silences. The problem took one hour to find: her CV spoke NGO language to tech recruiters. We translated the same experience — "$2.1M multi-country programme, 14 partners, 94% on-time delivery" — switched to direct outreach, and she signed an offer in week 8.

"The 312 applications were not wasted. They taught me exactly what was not working. The 8 weeks taught me what was."

23 Days · +$18,500
Marcus
Content Strategist · 31
$49,500 in-office$68,000remote-first + benefits

Four months, ~55 applications a month, 2% response rate, zero interviews. We cut applications to 10 a week, rewrote his headline around a real result ("2k → 140k monthly readers in 18 months"), built a one-page case-study PDF, and started 5 direct outreach messages a day. 23 days later: 4 discovery calls, 2 interviews, 1 offer.

A one-page PDF that looked like work — not a job application — changed the response rate most.
